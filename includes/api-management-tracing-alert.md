> [!WARNING]
> * Only enable tracing on subscriptions intended for debugging purposes.
> * Subscription keys that enable tracing should not be share with unauthorized users. Doing so could lead to disclosure of sensitive and secret information contained in tracing logs, such as keys, access tokens, passwords, internal hostnames, and IP addresses.
