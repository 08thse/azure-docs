1. Sign in to the [Azure portal](https://portal.azure.com).
1. Search for **Cost Management + Billing**.  
    :::image type="content" source="./media/billing-check-account-type/billing-search-cost-management-billing.png" alt-text="Screenshot that shows an Azure portal search for Cost Management + Billing." lightbox="./media/billing-check-account-type/billing-search-cost-management-billing.png" :::
1. If you have access to only one billing scope, select **Properties** from the leftmost pane.  
    :::image type="content" source="./media/billing-check-account-type/billing-mca-property.png" alt-text="Screenshot that shows a Microsoft Customer Agreement on the Properties pane." lightbox="./media/billing-check-account-type/billing-mca-property.png" :::
    The **Billing account type** on the **Properties** pane determines the type of your account. It can be Microsoft Online Service Program, Enterprise Agreement, Microsoft Customer Agreement, or Microsoft Partner Agreement. To learn more about the type of billing accounts, see [View your billing accounts in the Azure portal](../articles/cost-management-billing/manage/view-all-accounts.md).
1. If you have access to multiple billing scopes, check the type in the **Billing account type** column.
    :::image type="content" source="./media/billing-check-account-type/billing-account-type-in-the-list.png" alt-text="Screenshot that shows a Microsoft Customer Agreement in the Billing account type list." lightbox="./media/billing-check-account-type/billing-account-type-in-the-list.png" :::
