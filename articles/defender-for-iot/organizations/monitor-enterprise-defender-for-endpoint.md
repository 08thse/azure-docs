# Tutorial: Monitor Enterprise IoT networks with Defender for Endpoint and Defender for IoT

This article describes how to integrate Microsoft Defender for Endpoint with Microsoft Defender for IoT. This integration extends Defender for Endpoint's security monitoring with increased visibility 
This article describes how you can integrate Microsoft Defender for Endpoint with Microsoft Defender for IoT to monitor Enterprise IoT devices across your network.

Defender for Endpoint provides alerts, vulnerabilities, and recommendations for your enterprise devices. Defender for IoT supports the entire breadth of IoT devices in your environment, from corporate printers and cameras, to purpose-built, proprietary, and unique devices. Together, Defender for Endpoint and Defender for IoT provide complete coverage and security monitoring for your Enterprise IoT devices.

Microsoft Defender for Endpoint now seamlessly integrates with Microsoft Defender for IoT. This integration extends your device discovery capabilities with the agentless monitoring capabilities provided by Defender for IoT. The Defender for IoT integration provides increased visibility to help locate, identify, and secure the enterprise IoT devices in your network, such as Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) devices, printers, and cameras.

This allows organizations to take advantage of a single integrated solution that secures all of their IoT, and Operational Technology (OT) infrastructure. For more information, see Enterprise IoT network protection.

The Defender for IoT integration gives you a single unified view of your complete OT/IoT inventory alongside the rest of your IT devices (workstations, servers, and mobile). Customers who've onboarded to Defender for IoT will also get information on alerts, vulnerabilities and security recommendations for their IoT devices.

In this tutorial, you learn how to:

> [!div class="checklist"]
> * Onboard to Defender for IoT from Defender for Endpoint
> * View an updated device inventory with Enterprise IoT devices
> * Monitor your network using Defender for Endpoint alerts, vulnerabilities, and recommendations
